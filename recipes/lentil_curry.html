Lentil Curry

This is a sumptious vegan curry that is not only packed full of flavour but a good serving of protein as well.

Ingredients

  • Red Lentils
  • Coconut Milk
  • Green Chillies
  • Spices
  • Ginger
  • Fresh Coriander
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Chop all fresh ingredients, exept coriander
  2. Sautee ginger, garlice and chillies in pan
  3. Add spices and fry on medium heat for a minute
  4. Add in washed lentils and chopped tomates
  5. Cook for 20 minutes covered
  6. Add in coconut milk
  7. Let simmer for 5 minutes uncovered
  8. Garnish with coriander
